Know about Royal Challengers Bengaluru's Achievements :
  • IPL Champions in 2025 (Biggest Achievement)/ RCB women's Team 2 time Champions

The Royal Challengers Bengaluru men’s team created history by winning their first IPL title in 2025, ending a long wait and fulfilling the dreams of their fans after many near misses.
The Royal Challengers Bangalore Women women’s team has been even more successful, winning the Women’s Premier League (WPL) title twice, showing strong performance and consistency in a short time.
Together, both teams represent passion, resilience, and the growing success of RCB in Indian cricket.

  • Record-breaking batting performances :

In the Indian Premier League, the team has reached the finals three times (2009, 2011, and 2016) and holds one of the highest team totals in league history (263/5 in 2013). A major individual record is held by Virat Kohli, who scored 973 runs in the 2016 season—the most runs by any player in a single IPL edition. The team has also been part of many high-scoring and record-breaking matches over the years.
